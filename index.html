The Informational Build-up Framework

What if reality is made of information, not matter?

The Informational Build-up Framework (IBF) offers a fresh lens on reality, positioning information, not matter, at its core. Through this perspective, IBF proposes a radically new cosmology: one that is alive and not mechanical, and not static, but recursive. IBF envisions a universe inherently directional, where coherence continuously forms, dissolves, and reforms. Here, complexity and consciousness aren't in competition but arise naturally from the same underlying dynamic.

IBF elegantly reframes:

  • Reality as an informational continuum; cosmology as dynamic informational evolution;
  • Consciousness as something pre-built into this continuum, always guiding things toward coherence;
  • Matter as what happens when bits of this continuum settle into stable form;
  • Time not as a dimension, but the difference between states of this continuum;
  • Evolution, just a process of finding better fits; resonance tuning itself over time;
  • Causality, more like the trail coherence leaves behind than the thing that drives it;
  • Complexity, not built from chaos, but layered through alignment;
  • Intelligence as the ability to ride these layers; sensing, syncing, and shaping what resonates;
  • Humans not as separate observers, but as the continuum becoming aware of itself;
  • And reality, not fixed, not forced, but flowing, assembling itself through preference, pattern, and pull ...

...and many more fundamental concepts.

About the Author

Obsessed with the nature of reality and the meaning of information since childhood, Radu Negulescu chose the entrepreneurial path over academia, seeing it as the most direct way to test ideas against the world. Over the past two decades, he has built and led ventures across information technology and communications: from building national-scale critical systems and globally acclaimed products, to championing political campaigns & strategic communication projects world-wide. His work bridges technology, communication, and design, all driven by a single pursuit: reducing the entropy of human experience and making life more joyful.

The Informational Build-up Framework is his most ambitious contribution to date; a unifying model of reality meant to help individuals and societies navigate complexity with more intelligence, coherence, and freedom.
