The Informational Build-up Framework

Reality as resonance, not matter.

The Informational Build-up Framework (IBF) offers a fresh lens on reality, positioning information—not matter—at its core. Through this perspective, IBF proposes a radically new cosmology: one that is not mechanical but alive, not static but recursive. It envisions a universe inherently directional, where coherence continuously forms, dissolves, and reforms. Here, complexity and consciousness aren't in competition but arise naturally from the same underlying dynamic.

IBF elegantly reframes:

  • Consciousness as intrinsic informational resonance.
  • Complexity as recursive coherence-building.
  • Cosmology as dynamic informational evolution.

...and many more fundamental concepts.

Immediate Possible Applications:

  • Aligning Artificial Intelligence with human values.
  • Rethinking quantum mechanics through informational coherence.
  • Developing novel models for understanding consciousness and complexity.
